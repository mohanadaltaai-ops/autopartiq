# PartLink IQ / PartLink AE - Store Listing Draft

## Google Play Short Description

Request car parts in minutes and receive offers from verified suppliers.

## Google Play Full Description

PartLink IQ and PartLink AE are mobile-first spare parts marketplace applications built to connect customers with verified auto parts suppliers.

Customers can submit one spare parts request, add vehicle details, describe the required part, and receive competitive offers from suppliers. Suppliers can review matching leads, submit prices, and manage accepted orders. Operations teams can monitor orders, suppliers, payments, delivery status, and settlements through dedicated admin roles.

Key features:
- Submit spare parts requests quickly
- Add vehicle make, model, year, origin, and part details
- Receive offers from verified suppliers
- Review offer price, delivery information, and supplier details
- Accept the best offer
- Track order progress
- Save frequently used vehicle details
- Supplier lead and offer management
- Admin order and supplier management
- Market-specific experience for Iraq and UAE

PartLink IQ is designed for the Iraq market.
PartLink AE is designed for the United Arab Emirates market.

## Apple App Store Description

PartLink helps customers find vehicle spare parts by connecting them with verified suppliers in their market.

Instead of contacting multiple shops manually, customers can submit one request with vehicle and part details. Suppliers receive matching leads and provide offers. Customers can compare offers, accept the most suitable option, and track the order.

The app supports customer, supplier, and operations workflows, including requests, offers, orders, saved vehicles, notifications, and admin management.

PartLink IQ serves the Iraq market.
PartLink AE serves the United Arab Emirates market.
