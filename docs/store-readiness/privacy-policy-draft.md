# Privacy Policy Draft - PartLink IQ / PartLink AE

Last updated: [Insert Date]

This Privacy Policy explains how PartLink IQ and PartLink AE collect, use, store, and protect user information.

## 1. Who We Are

PartLink IQ and PartLink AE are spare parts marketplace applications that connect customers, suppliers, and operations teams.

PartLink IQ serves users in Iraq.  
PartLink AE serves users in the United Arab Emirates.

## 2. Information We Collect

Depending on your role and how you use the app, we may collect:

- Account information such as phone number, email address, role, market, and login details
- Vehicle information such as make, model, year, origin, and saved vehicle preferences
- Spare parts request details such as part name, description, photos, quantity, and delivery requirements
- Supplier offer details such as price, availability, delivery time, notes, and order status
- Order information such as accepted offers, delivery status, payment status, and operational notes
- Notifications and app activity related to requests, offers, orders, and account actions
- Technical information needed to operate and secure the app

## 3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Create and manage user accounts
- Authenticate users securely
- Match customer requests with relevant suppliers
- Allow suppliers to submit offers
- Allow customers to accept offers and track orders
- Support admin and operations workflows
- Send service-related notifications
- Improve app reliability, safety, and performance
- Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
- Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

## 4. Sharing of Information

We may share limited information between app users where needed for the marketplace to function.

Examples:

- Suppliers may see relevant customer request and vehicle details
- Customers may see supplier offer details
- Admin and operations users may access order, supplier, request, and settlement information

We do not sell user personal data.

We may use trusted third-party service providers for hosting, authentication, database services, OTP/email delivery, analytics, error monitoring, and other app operations.

## 5. Data Retention

We keep user data for as long as needed to provide the service, operate the marketplace, resolve disputes, maintain records, prevent fraud, and meet legal or regulatory requirements.

Some order, transaction, settlement, audit, or compliance records may need to be retained even after an account deletion request.

## 6. Account Deletion

Users may request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

Deletion requests can be submitted through:

- In-app account deletion request flow: [To be implemented / confirmed]
- Email: support@autopartiq.com for IQ users
- Email: support@autopartsae.com for AE users

When we receive a deletion request, we will review and process it within a reasonable period. Some data may be retained where required for legal, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, financial, audit, or regulatory reasons.

## 7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user information, including access controls, authentication, and secure hosting practices.

No system is completely secure, and users should keep their login details and devices protected.

## 8. Children

The app is intended for business and general marketplace use and is not directed to children.
